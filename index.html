Bear

A bear is a large mammal found in forests, mountains, and tundras. They are known for their thick fur, powerful build, and sharp claws. Bears are omnivorous, eating both plants and animals, and are often solitary creatures.

Lion

A lion is a big cat, often called the "king of the jungle." They live in grasslands and savannas and are known for their majestic mane (in males) and social behavior, living in groups called prides. Lions are carnivores and are apex predators in their habitat.

Rabbit

A rabbit is a small, furry mammal with long ears and strong hind legs. They are herbivores, feeding on grass, leaves, and vegetables. Rabbits are known for their quick movements and burrowing habits, and they reproduce rapidly.
